* What is it? *

The basic definition is that schools serve precisely to train dogs, if the owner does not have much time available in his day-to-day to do so..
When talking exclusively about dog schools, owners are usually unaware of their existence and functioning.
But, exactly what the name promises, it is a school, similar to the ones where children go, spend the day studying and return home.

* Learning at School *


The school, which is aimed at the canine public, has some activities related to dog training. But despite this, students are entitled to nap time,
playtime and snack time. They often also offer more relaxing activities like massage, swimming lessons and games.
* Benefits *
Both for puppies and older dogs, schools offer comfort for all types of age, always taking care of the care and needs of each dog.
From ramps, toys, pool baths, massages and nap time, all dogs can participate in what is proposed in the plan, to improve not only their education but
also provide them with company during the day.
